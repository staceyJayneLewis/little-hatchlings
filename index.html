About Us

Parents like you have been placing their children in our nurseries for nearly 10 years. We started off as one business in South Wales, and now we have many different nurseries based all around Wales that are all individual and designed to serve the needs of each community, which is why we have been voted the No. 1 nursery in Wales. We are a family-run business that is highly qualified in the childcare industry and has taken on the opportunity to recruit the most qualified and trusted staff to care for our little hatchlings.
